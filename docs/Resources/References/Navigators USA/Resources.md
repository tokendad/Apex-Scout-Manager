## Organizational Hierarchy

**National Board of Directors**: Oversees the national budget, insurance, and resource development. The board includes founders and experienced chapter leaders.
**National Office / Support Team**: Provides administrative support, processes Chapter Applications, and manages national partnerships (e.g., the Heart & Soul Charitable Fund).
**Local Chapters**: The primary functional unit. Each chapter is led by at least two volunteer leaders and must have a minimum of five children.
**Chapter Members**: Families and youth who participate in the program. 

#### Programmatic Structure (By Age)
`The youth members are organized into three main sections based on age: `

- Stargazers: Ages 4–6.
- Junior Navigators: Ages 7–10, 
    - Mira
    - Vega
    - Polaris
- Senior Navigators: Ages 11–18
    - Shadow
    - Tracker
    - Pilot
    - Navigator
#### Leadership Roles at the Chapter Level
- Chapter Leaders: Two required adults who facilitate activities and manage local logistics.
- Parents/Volunteers: Encouraged to participate in a "whole family" model rather than a "drop-off" program.
- Quartermaster: A specific role often designated to manage chapter gear and supplies.
